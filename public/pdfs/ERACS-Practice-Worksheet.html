Step 3: Apply ERACS

Question every detail and develop the new method using ERACS.

Guiding Questions:

  • Why is it necessary? What is its purpose?
  • Where/when should it be done? Who is best qualified?
  • How is the best way to do it?
  • Are we using the right: Machine? Equipment? Tools? Layout? Movement?
